Hi, I’m Thearoth, a dedicated student currently studying at RUPP, and I am pursuing my passion for web design as a junior at ISTAD. Over time, I have developed a strong foundation in various technical and design skills, including HTML, CSS, Tailwind CSS, C/C++, and UX/UI design. My journey in web development began with learning HTML, which taught me how to structure web pages effectively, and CSS, which allowed me to bring those pages to life with visually appealing designs and layouts. I further enhanced my workflow by mastering Tailwind CSS, a utility-first framework that enables me to create responsive and customizable designs efficiently. In addition to my web development skills, I have also gained experience in C and C++, which have helped me build a solid understanding of programming fundamentals, problem-solving, and algorithmic thinking. While these languages are not directly used in web development, they have provided me with a strong technical foundation that I can apply to other areas of programming and development. On the design side, I have delved into UX/UI principles, learning how to create user-friendly and visually engaging interfaces. This involves understanding user behavior, designing wireframes and prototypes, and ensuring that websites are not only functional but also aesthetically pleasing and easy to navigate. My goal is to combine my technical skills with my design knowledge to create websites that offer seamless and enjoyable user experiences. I am passionate about using my skills to build interactive and responsive websites that cater to the needs of users while maintaining a high standard of design and functionality. Whether it’s crafting a clean and modern layout, optimizing a site for performance, or ensuring accessibility across devices, I am committed to delivering high-quality digital solutions. As I continue to grow and learn, I am excited to take on new challenges and further refine my expertise in web design and development.
